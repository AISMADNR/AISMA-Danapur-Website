About AISMA Danapur Division

The All India Station Masters' Association (AISMA) represents station masters across the Indian Railways. The Danapur Division is an important part of the East Central Railway zone, and AISMA works to uphold the interests and welfare of station masters here.

Our association aims to promote professionalism, safety, and camaraderie among station masters and support their career and personal growth.
